Replaces /research (the old Overview) and /research/history (the old Queries → History page) with a single route. KPI strip lives once, not twice. Active runs are a pinned section at the top of the same ledger that holds the rest of the archive — same row schema, same sort/filter affordances. Trend triplet (activity · verdicts · status mix) replaces the four full-width charts that used to sit between Overview's panels and the user's actual work. Compose remains the unambiguous top of the page.

Mockup uses Material Palenight palette and the app's Noto Sans / Merriweather / Noto Sans Mono stack. Counts and dates illustrative.
