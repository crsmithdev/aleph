02 · Observability control bar

Hover-expand controls

Observability pages share one control bar at the top: title, dataset, filters, options, then range and granularity. Each group hovers open into a segmented control; collapsed it shows the current value with a small icon. This compresses a lot of state into one row without ever feeling crowded.

03 · Empty states & system messages

Sentences, not illustrations

Empty states are a single line, sometimes with a hint where to go next. No mascots, no full-bleed art. Toasts use a 3px colored left border to carry status and a single icon — never a hero color flood.
